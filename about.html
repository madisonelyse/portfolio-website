About Madison

My Story

Hi, I’m Madison! I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in client advocacy and project management. I believe in providing simple solutions to complex problems through effective communication and research. I transitioned to UX/UI design because I have a knack for empathizing with people from multiple vantage points and catering to individual needs to provide all-inclusive environments. I want to work with a like-minded team to produce user-centered deliverables that add value and ease to people’s lives.
